The Secret of Monkey Island

The Secret of Monkey Island is a point-and-click adventure game full of pirates, voodoo and oddball humour. You play as Guybrush Threepwood, a young man (with alledgedly fancy pants), trying to become a pirate on the fictional Caribbean island of Mêlée. Whilst attempting to undertake three pirate trials to earn acceptance as a filthy swashbuckler, Guybrush inadvertently uncovers a plot to kidnap the island's governor and clumsily tries to rescue her. But it's not just any scurvy pirates involved; the legendary Ghost Pirate LeChuck is the culprit! Guybrush will need more than a cheap t-shirt to defeat the infamous ghost and his haunted crew.
